Methodology

  1. Input profile and optional lab text.
  2. Extract hormone markers (TSH, T3, T4, HbA1c, insulin, cortisol, cholesterol, fasting glucose).
  3. Apply weighted risk scoring and correlation logic.
  4. Return risk percentages, risk levels, key triggers, recommended actions, and suggested tests.
  5. Store assessment in secure admin records.

Correlation examples: poor sleep + high stress, high BMI + diabetes family history, and irregular cycles + insulin issues.
